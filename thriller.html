About the book

The Skeleton Key

Kelly's latest novel, The Skeleton Key, is a gripping psychological thriller that explores the dark secrets of two families tied together by history and marriage. The novel is inspired by the author's love for Masquerade and features a literal golden key and a literal human skeleton. It is a taut, mesmerizing novel of danger and obsession that will keep you on the edge of your seat.

About the Author

Erin Kelly

Erin Kelly is a British author and journalist born in London in 1976. She grew up in Romford, Essex, and read English at Warwick University. Kelly has been working as a journalist since 1998, writing for newspapers such as The Sunday Times, The Sunday Telegraph, the Daily Mail, and the Express, as well as magazines including Red, Psychologies, Marie Claire, Elle, and Cosmopolitan. She is also a creative writing tutor.

Fiction Book Club

In Memoriam
by Alice Winn

A poignant and heartfelt exploration of loss, love, and the enduring power of memories. Through beautifully crafted prose, Winn invites readers into the deeply personal journey of a grieving protagonist as she navigates the complexities of grief and attempts to find solace in the memories of a loved one. This emotionally charged novel is a testament to the resilience of the human spirit and the profound impact of those we hold dear. "In Memoriam" is a moving literary work that delves into the universal experience of mourning while celebrating the enduring bonds that connect us to the past.

Non-Fiction Book Club

Agatha Christies
by Lucy Worsley

A superlative biography of the Queen of Crime from one of our most noted historians, Worsley's page-turning volume emphasises not just Christie's unique gifts as a storyteller but her pioneering qualities as a determined, successful and thoroughly modern woman.

Science-Fiction Book Club

Creation Node
by Stephen Baxter

Baxter's latest novel, Creation Node, is a science fiction novel set in the year 2255. The novel follows the discovery of an object called Planet Nine, which turns out to be something not quite human. The novel is a taut, mesmerizing novel of danger and obsession that will keep you on the edge of your seat.

Thriller Book Club

The Skeleton Key
by Erin Kelly

Kelly's latest novel, The Skeleton Key, is a gripping psychological thriller that explores the dark secrets of two families tied together by history and marriage. The novel is inspired by the author's love for Masquerade and features a literal golden key and a literal human skeleton. It is a taut, mesmerizing novel of danger and obsession that will keep you on the edge of your seat.
